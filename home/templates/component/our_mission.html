Our Mission

Ensuring that every individual in deprived communities has the opportunity to access quality education and training to reach full potential and reduce social vices.

ORGANIZATIONAL HISTORY

After three years of operation, the organization has seven diversified Board of Directors with the founder who serves as Executive Director. The board also defines policies and expected program outcomes and monitors effectiveness as well as participates actively in the activities of the organization. We also have a Finance Officer, Project coordinator, administrative officer, Monitoring and Evaluation Officer, Gender Empowerment and Social Inclusion Officer, and a Volunteer Network of fifty (50) membership.
