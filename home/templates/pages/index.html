Empowering Girls

We are a grass-root non-profit organization striving to ensure that every child regardless of their geographical location has the opportunity to acquire literacy skills, access to books, digital skills,the right to social justice and youth empowerment in order to assist them in their quest to maximize their potential for success. The LGF has a fundamental duty to educate, inspire and provide skill training to young people. This is especially directed at girls in underprivileged communities where this has been dwarfed by poverty and outmoded cultural practices. We as a foundation are committed to the welfare of the youth and children. We believe occurrences such as teenage pregnancy and drug abuse are caused by young people who never had the fortune of a proper education. LGF embraces this responsibility to provide opportunities to all without discrimination on the grounds of their age, culture, nationality, creed, ethnicity, disability, gender, language, racial origin, or religious belief
Our mission i: ensuring that every individual in deprived communities has the opportunity to access quality education and training to reach full potential and reduce social vices.
LGF’s vision is to spearhead the growth of literacy, access to books, gender equality and social justice in communities in Ghana through dynamic, innovative and practical development programs.
