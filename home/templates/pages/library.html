LEARNERS GIRLS FOUNDATION LIBRARY

View Gallery

Learners Girls Foundation, a dedicated non-governmental organization, has introduced a pioneering library and e-learning center in Paga, situated within the Kassena-Nankana West District of the Upper East Region. This exceptional facility represents a major step in the organization's mission to empower girls and promote education within the community.

The library, the first of its kind in the region, has been meticulously curated to cater to the diverse educational needs of the local population. It houses an extensive collection of books spanning various academic fields, ensuring that students and learners of all ages have access to a wealth of knowledge. The facility also includes a dedicated e-learning section, equipped with computers and digital resources that facilitate contemporary teaching and mentoring methods.

The inspiration for this remarkable project emerged during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic when schools were forced to close, disrupting traditional learning methods. The Learners Girls Foundation, concurrently engaged in a noble initiative offering free tuition to students to mitigate the pandemic's impact on education in the area, encountered a challenge with providing adequate space for learning.

To address this challenge, the foundation secured crucial support from the African Publishing Innovation Fund, an initiative of the International Publishers Association. This financial assistance enabled the organization to transform its vision into a reality, providing a sanctuary for learning and exploration. Key partners in this initiative include World Vision Ghana, who provided essential furniture, ensuring the library is a welcoming and comfortable space, and the Ghana Publishers Association, who supplied an extensive array of books, enriching the library's collection.

The Learners Girls Foundation Library embodies a deep commitment to educational access and empowerment in the region. This facility is poised to create meaningful opportunities and foster positive impacts on the community's future by emphasizing the crucial role of knowledge in empowerment. The organization is excited about the possibilities it will offer and the positive influence it will exert on the aspirations and potential of countless individuals as they embark on their learning journey within its walls.
