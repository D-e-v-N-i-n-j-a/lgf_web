PARTNERSHIPS AND COLLOBORATIONS

The organization won the AFRICAN PUBLISHING INNOVATION FUND in 2021, an initiative of the international publishers’ association with which a project dubbed ‘Girl Child Education and Empowerment Through Mentorship and E-Learning Initiative’ was executed for the marginalized in Paga through the construction of a library stocked with 2000+ books including locally published content . Also, there is an E-learning component for a digitally accessed Mentorship Program. The lives of about 500 young girls have been impacted with our STEM mentorship support initiative which has contributed to most of these girls enrolling in STEM programs in the universities. The Learner Guide Initiative; The APIF grant has also helped promote literacy through our learner guide initiative in five schools and two educational empowerment camps to facilitate reading and writing.
Problem statement

Within our community, a pressing concern emerges as a result of limited access to education for children. This challenge is rooted in barriers such as inadequate infrastructure, lack of qualified teachers, and limited learning resources.

CONTINUOUS PARTNERSHIPS

The organization is currently working in partnership with the District Level Partnership in the Ghana Education Service. The organization has created a working partnership with local key stakeholders such as the GHANA EDUCATION SERVICE, GIRLS EMPOWERMENT MISSION GHANA and CAMFED ASSOCIATION DISTRICT LEVEL NETWORK
