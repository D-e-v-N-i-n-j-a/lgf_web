Goals and Objectives

Improve Learning Outcomes

The primary goal of LGF is to enhance the learning outcomes of students, specifically targeting 2000 students (upper primary and junior high school levels) annually in the Upper East and Northern regions of Ghana. This objective aims to raise the overall educational achievement of these students.

Promote Reading Diversity

LGF intends to expose learners to a wide variety of book genres, authors, and even languages. By doing so, students will gain exposure to diverse literary content, broadening their horizons and language skills.

Enhance Communication Skills

One of the specific objectives is to help children and students build effective communication skills. Book talks provide a platform for students to discuss and articulate their thoughts about books, which can significantly improve their speaking and communication abilities.
